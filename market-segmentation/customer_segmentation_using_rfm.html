Calculating RFM Score, RFM score is calculated based upon recency, frequency, monetary value normalize ranks. Based upon this score we divide our customers. Here we rate them on a scale of 5. Formula used for calculating rfm score is : 0.15Recency score + 0.28Frequency score + 0.57 *Monetary score¶
